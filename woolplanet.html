Wool Planet
Illustration · Personal Project · 2014
Project Summary
Wool planet is an illustration project for a fairytale book. This book is about a cat and a guardian who had to send the cat to heaven. I imagined when the farewell comes to them, all the cats go to the planet called wool planet. It’s like an after world. The flow of the story contains farewell, meet again and get old together.
