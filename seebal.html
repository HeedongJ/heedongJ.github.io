SeeBal
Editorial ·Photography · Personal Project · 2019
Project Prologue
SeeBal is a photography book started from 2 keywords: avocado and shadow. The word ‘Avocado’ is a slang word that indicates a homosexual who pretends to be heterosexual. Because it looks like a vegetable, but it’s actually a fruit. The shadow means something’s dark side that makes contrast to the bright side. So sometimes the word ‘shadow’ is expressed as our bad experience and memory that we want to conceal.
Intention
Those kind of words are used by people who think they have more priority in the society to justify their status. So the word is like an arrow. Ignore it or not, it flies towards somebody. If it is good, it goes to the head, if it is bad, it goes to the heart.
