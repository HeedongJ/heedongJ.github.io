Regrets
Category / Editorial
Year / 2019
Size / 110x190(mm)

Regrets is a work that began with "everything has double sided-ness". A friend, who has been my best friend for a long time, has been confused about her different appearances on internet and in reality. I wanted her to be a little more free from the eyes of others. But there was nothing I could do.
Project Description
Bonsai refers to a small plant made solely to be displayed. A well-groomed bonsai fills the viewer with wonder. However, this should be contrasted with growing it in a repressed environment. This double sided symbolism of beauty and oppression of bonsai was expressed using sewing techniques. When the book is opened, it consists of pages with images and text only.Text is a list of conflicting messages that have nothing to do with the images, which acts as an element that interferes with the flow of the book or throws questions.
