TDL Series
Category / Editorial
Year / 2019
TDL series is an abbreviation of Time, Date, and Place, which is a project about time difference.The reason we feel jet lag is because two different times collide. Panoramic picture with errors show this characteristic well. When you take a panoramic picture, there is a time difference between the beginning and end of the frame as you move from right to left or from left to right. I defined that the error occurred as a result of the collision of two different times. Traveling for five years, I've archived errored panoramic pictures, and I want to explore connections to the time gap between the time zone I live in and the new place where I feel time difference.
About TDL Series
The TDL series consists of three parts, two edits and one poster.
