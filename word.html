Dirty Korean!
Infographic · Personal Project · 2019
About Project
Every year many new words which reflect period and society appear.
Sometimes they disappear as easily. As our world development is getting faster and faster, this ‘new word generating phenomenon’ is also accelerated. As a consequence of thoughtlessly generated words, social issue occur, for example: conversation conflict between generations, insult of socially disadvantaged people or people who have specific role, etc. This issue caught my eye and I somehow felt a duty to talk about it and express it in my way. These kind of words have sensational character which is hard to forget once we hear or read them. For my infographic project, I used a neon sign as the graphic motive. It is flashing, blinking and easily disappearing like a newly coined word. For conveying the content effectively, I chose web(internet) environment, where newly coined words are mostly used.
