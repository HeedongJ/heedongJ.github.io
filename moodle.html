Moodle
Artwork · Personal Project · 2019
Project Summary
Have you ever stayed immersed in lingering imagery
when a movie ended?
In that sense, a scent is like a movie.There is this sentence in the novel The Great Gatsby: "Daisy's Yellow Cocktail Laughter". When I first read this sentence, I got an illusion that the scent was hovering around the tip of my nose.The movie plays for a fixed time, and the candle burns for a fixed time. In that sense, the moodle is a scented candle that burns with different scents according to the narrative and timeline of the movie.This project is a 4D experiment that attempts to combine vision and smell.
Project Process
The 23mm diameter candle burns about 11 mm per 20 minutes. Depending on the narrative structure of the movie or the change in the emotions of the characters, the movie was divided into three parts: unfolding, climax, and the ending. Short films were divided into two parts.The appropriate scent was selected for each part. The length of each part was calculated to match the timing of the story transitions. Depending on the timeline of the movie, the candle burns and the scent that fits the scene is emitted.
Love Letter
Moodle Collection
Moodle is a compound word of movie and candle.The Moodle set consists of candles produced for 6 movies.The length of candles varies depending on the running time. Not to interfere with the immersion in a movie, the basic form of candles, the cylindrical form, was chosen and colors picked from the movie were added.
